About Me


Over the years I have gained years of experience in many areas of the catering industry. From a young age I worked tirelessly to get to where I am today. Start from Deeside College, studying catering and work my way from junior chef to head chef position at very young age.

After working in the industry for over 10 years and winning many awards included Chef of the Year 2017 at Upstair at the Grill.

Read More

I founded Private Chef Services, to bring some of premium restaurants service to the weddings, dinner parties and social events. Based in Bromley, I cater for private and corporate events across England and Wales. The "wow-factor" is the most important part of the process. Seeing the joy and elation on the faces of his guests is what fuels my passion for cooking.

I offers a delicious Pan-Asian style menu, bursting with bold and blended flavours. It's a must for anybody with a passion for discovering fresh, vibrant and forward-thinking menus!

Private Chef

Private chef hire is ideal if you want to make the most of your time with your guests. I caters for groups of up to 25, providing a personal chef service for private dinner party catering, hen dos, holiday lets, family and business gatherings or just a relaxing evening with friends. I bring the restaurant experience to you, providing delicious menu’s in the comfort of your own home or holiday let.

Read More

I offers professional, reliable and flexible service. Choose your favourite menu from me and taste interesting flavours from around the world. Know that everything is freshly prepared by a professional chef. Add your own special requests, children’s options or dietary requirements. Forget the hassle – everything is cooked, served and cleared away for you.

I will takes care of everything, from purchasing quality ingredients sourced locally, preparing the meal in your kitchen and then tidying up the kitchen before leaving. It lets you fully enjoy the company of your guests, whether the meal takes place with family, friends or business relations.
