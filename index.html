About Me


Chef Dicson has gained years of experience in many areas of the Catering industry. From a young age he has worked tirelessly to get to where he is today. Start from Deeside College, studying catering and work his way from junior chef to head chef position at very young age. Now he leads Professional and Private Chef Services from the kitchen, maintaining a very hands on approach to professional catering.

After working in the industry for over 10 years and winning many awards included Chef of the Year 2017 (At Upstair at the Grill). He founded Private Chef Services, to bring some of premium reseraunt service to the weddings, dinner parties and social events. Based in Bromley, we cater for private and corporate events across England and Wales. The ‘wow-factor’ is the most important part of the process for Chef Dicson. Seeing the joy and elation on the faces of his guests is what fuels his passion for cooking.

Chef Dicson offers a delicious Pan-Asian style menu, bursting with bold and blended flavours. With a hand selected wine, it's a must for anybody with a passion for discovering fresh, vibrant and forward-thinking menus!

Private Chef


Private chef hire is ideal if you want to make the most of your time with your guests. Chef Dicson caters for groups of up to 50, providing a personal chef service for private dinner party catering, hen dos, holiday lets, family and business gatherings or just a relaxing evening with friends. We bring the restaurant to you, providing delicious menu’s in the comfort of your own home or holiday let.

We are professional, reliable and flexible service. Choose your favourite menu from us and taste interesting flavours from around the world. Know that everything is freshly prepared by a professional chef. Add your own special requests, children’s options or dietary requirements. Forget the hassle – everything is cooked, served and cleared away for you.

Chef Dicson will takes care of everything, from purchasing quality ingredients sourced locally, preparing the meal in your kitchen and then tidying up the kitchen before leaving. It lets you fully enjoy the company of your guests, whether the meal takes place with family, friends or business relations.
